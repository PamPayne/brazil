# ORDINANCE SPA/MF No. 1,225, OF 31st OF JULY OF 2024

**FEDERAL OFFICIAL GAZETTE**  
Published on: 01/08/2024 | Edition: 147 | Section: 1 | Page: 73  
Entity: Ministry of Finance/Secretariat of Prizes and Bets

**ORDINANCE SPA/MF No. 1,225, OF 31st OF JULY OF 2024**

On the regulations for the monitoring and investigation of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents, treated by Laws No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, and No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023.

THE SECRETARY OF PRIZES AND BETS OF THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE, in the exercise of powers bestowed upon him by art. 55, item I, paragraph “d”, of Annex I of Decree No. 11,907, of 30th of January 2024, and considering the provisions on art. 29, § 3, of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December 2018, and on Chapter IX of Law No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023, rules:

## CHAPTER I

## PRELIMINARY PROVISIONS

Art. 1.- This Ordinance regulates monitoring and investigation of activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents, treated by Laws No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, and No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023.

Art. 2.- For the purposes of this Ordinance, the following terms are considered as follows:

- I - **monitoring**: set of activities for systematic and continuous tracking, executed through data, information and document analysis, aimed at verifying compliance of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds betting and betting operator agents with the legal regulations and guidelines or detecting risks of non-compliance, including as support for the investigation;
- II - **investigation**: set of specific activities, including inspection, that involves documents, information and data access, retrieval and finding, aimed at perfecting the orderliness of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds betting and betting operator agents, compliance with legal duties and guidelines, and eventual resolution of previously identified risks;
- III - **inspection**: activity that may occur during investigation and shall be executed remotely or on site, aimed at assessing and validating specific aspects of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds betting and betting operator agents, detecting failures and ensuring compliance with the legal regulations and guidelines;
- IV - **preventive and cautionary measures**: measures deemed necessary and urgent to eliminate, reduce, control or mitigate identified risks;
- V - **investigation team**: team in charge of performing the investigation of activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents;
- VI - **inspection form**: document used by the investigation team to record data, information and standards that were identified during the inspection;
- VII - **investigation report**: document issued by the investigation team which describes the procedures followed, the analysis performed, the obtained results and the measures proposed upon the ending of the investigation actions; and
- VIII - **information request**: ruling that demands the presentation of information deemed relevant for monitoring and investigation.

## CHAPTER II

## ON MONITORING AND INVESTIGATION

### Section I

### General Provisions

Art. 3.- Monitoring and investigation of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents will have a national coverage, under the responsibility of the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance, in the exercise of investigation competencies.

§ 1. The Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance will have the capacity to coordinate with other public entities to investigate on the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets, in order to ensure the observance of legal norms and regulations.

§ 2. The investigation measures apply, as appropriate, to the activities of physical or legal persons that are not duly authorised by the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance to explore the lottery modality of fixed odds betting.

Art. 4.- Monitoring and investigation of activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents will observe the following conditions:

- I - acting based on evidence and risk management, oriented and focused on results;
- II - acting in a cohesively and in coordination among bodies and entities of the Ministry of Finance and other bodies and entities of public administration; and
- III - acting proportionally to the identified risks and coherently with the conduct of the betting operator agents.

### Section II

### On Monitoring

Art. 5.- Monitoring of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents will be continuously and systematically executed by the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance, and will entail the following monitoring modalities:

- I - of conduct, with the aim of supporting and analysing the apropriateness of the activities and betting operator agents to the legal norms and regulations; and
- II - precautionary, with the aim of validating the efficacy and effectivity of the system adopted by the betting operator agents regarding risk identification, valuation and treatment to mitigate threats to their usual operation, through a framework organised for timely intervention when necessary.

### Section III

### On Investigation

Art. 6.- Investigation of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents will be executed on a regular schedule, ex officio or by court order.

§ 1. Scheduled investigation will be executed through elaborate planning of the Office of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance based on risk management and evidence.

§ 2. Ex officio investigation will be triggered by the necessity of investigation as identified by the Office of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance or by formal communication to the public administration.

§ 3. Court ordered investigation, when triggered by formal communication to the public administration, must be preceded by fact checking, aimed at presenting signs of commission of an administrative infraction.

§ 4. Investigation actions must be recorded on the Electronic System of Information - SEI, in a process typified by their corresponding legal hypothesis.

Art. 7.- Investigation, once initiated, shall last as long as necessary to clarify the facts, observing Law No. 9,873, of 23rd of November of 1999.

### Section IV

### On Inspection

Art. 8.- Inspection is aimed at the examination and validation of specific aspects of the activities that explore lottery modalities of fixed odds bets and betting operator agents, as well as detection of failures and ensuring compliance with legal norms and regulations.

§ 1. The inspection may occur:

- I - in a physical environment, when it is deemed necessary to perform an in-situ assessment of materials, equipment and other resources employed by the betting operator agent for the exploration of fixed odds betting lottery modality; or
- II - remotely, through remote contact or connection to a remote device with safe and unrestricted access to the systems, platforms, data and other resources employed by the betting operator agent for the exploration of fixed odds betting lottery modality.

Art. 9.- Inspection activities must be initiated through an inspection form signed by the investigation team and signed by the investigated part or its legal representative.

§ 1. Absence of signature or refusal to sign the inspection form on the investigated part or its legal representative will not nullify the inspection performed, said situation must be recorded by the investigation team on the form.

§ 2. A copy of the inspection form may be handed to the investigated part or its legal representative, upon request to the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance.

Art. 10.- Remote inspection must observe information security management practices and it will be established in a manner that preserves and ensures the continuity of services, availability, timeliness, consistency, integrity, confidentiality and authenticity of data and information.

§ 1. Remote access must allow the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance to faithfully visualize and reproduce the data and information from the systems used by the betting operator agent, without it interfering on the sources of said systems.

§ 2. During remote inspection, the betting operator agent will be ensured to be informed about the execution of the procedure and traceability of the information accessed by the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance.

Art. 11.- Without prejudice to the remote access to the betting operator agent systems, remote inspection may also be executed through interviews, meetings and surveys, or any other manner of remote contact, such as videoconference.

§ 1. Partners of the betting operator agent will be subject to the inspection referred to on the caption.

§ 2. Collaborators, suppliers and service providers of the betting operator agent may be subject to the inspection referred to on the caption if they perform activities that are directly or indirectly related to the systems, platforms, data and other resources employed by the betting operator agent for the exploration of fixed odds betting lottery modality.

Art. 12.- It will be compulsory for the investigation team to identify themselves before the betting operator agent.

Single paragraph. The identification referred to on the caption will not be compulsory under the hypothesis that confidentiality is essential for the efficacy of the inspection or safety of the investigation team, which must be recorded on the investigation report.

### Section V

### On the Requisition for Information

Art. 13.- The requisition for information may occur at any time during monitoring and investigation, and must be formalized on a notification.

§ 1. Fulfilling the requisition referred to on the caption must happen in up to ten days since the reception of the notification.

§ 2. The requisition for information can be sent to the physical and electronic addresses registered for the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance or through Bet Management System - SIGAP.

§ 3. Failing to fulfill the requisition may result in the penalties provided by the specific regulations of the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance.

### Section VI

### On the Investigation Report

Art. 14.- Upon finalisation of each investigation, the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance will issue an investigation report containing:

- I - the investigation actions performed;
- II - the circumstances observed;
- III - the results obtained during the investigation, if it took place;
- IV - the analysis resulting from the investigation; and
- V - proposed guidelines resulting from the investigation.

Art. 15.- The investigation will be finalised with its corresponding report, that may result in an isolated or joint proposal to:

- I - archive the report;
- II - enforce preventive or cautionary measures;
- III - enforce corrective measures; and
- IV - initiate a sanctioning administrative proceeding:

§ 1. The report referred to on the caption will be forwarded to the Undersecretary of Monitoring and Investigation of the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance, who will rule on the measures to be adopted.

§ 2. When conduct that presents signs of a crime is identified, the investigation team will forward the notification to the competent bodies.

## CHAPTER III

## ON DATA AND INFORMATION ACCESS

Art. 16.- Within the framework of monitoring and investigation actions, the Secretary of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance may access data and information, as well as request, receive and obtain clarifications, technical, operational, economic-financial and accounting information, documents, certificates, attestations and reports from the betting operator agents through the following means, among others:

- I - regular consignment;
- II - remote inspection;
- III - in-situ inspection; and
- IV - requisition for information.

§ 1. Regular consignments must be done through Bet Management System - SIGAP or other system that may replace it, in compliance with legal norms and regulations.

§ 2. The means referred to on the caption may be used in parallel.

## CHAPTER IV

## ON HINDRANCE TO INVESTIGATION

Art. 17.- It constitutes a hindrance to investigation to deny or obstruct the access to data and information systems, not to show or provide bookkeeping documentation, records or papers, including electronically, within the terms, forms and conditions established by the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance on the execution of its investigation activity.

Single paragraph. The following are considered hypothesis of hindrance to investigation, among others:

- I - interposing hindrance to actions or rejecting to respond to the investigation team;
- II - failing to submit or incorrect and untimely submission of any requested data, information or document;
- III - submission of untruthful data, information and document or that may deliberately lead to misinterpretation of its content;
- IV - imposition of difficulties or obstruction to physical access to the facilities of the betting operator agent; or
- V - noncompliance with requisition for information.

Art. 18.- Once the hindrance to investigation is typified, the investigation team will propose the initiation of a sanctioning administrative proceeding, without prejudice to the necessary actions to continue with the investigation, to the imposition of ot her coercive and cautionary measures, and to the communication of potential signs of the commission of a crime to the competent authorities.

## CHAPTER V

## ON COERCIVE AND CAUTIONARY MEASURES

Art. 19.- The following measures may be applied, as precautionary, before the initiation or during the protocols for the sanctioning administrative proceeding, when the likelihood and concern of delay requirements are met, on an well-founded decision:

- I - temporary deactivation of instruments, equipment, systems or other objects and components allocated to the operation of machines and facilities;
- II - temporary suspension of payment of prizes;
- III - recall of tickets issued; and
- IV - other cautionary actions deemed necessary for the protection of the legal good.

Art. 20.- The betting operator agent will communicate about all signs of tampering of events or results that are identified or reported to the Secretary of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance and to the Ministry of Public Affairs.

Single paragraph. The communication referred to on the caption will occur within five working days since the date the betting operator agent identifies or becomes aware of the sign of tampering, through Bet Management System - SIGAP

Art. 21.- In the existence of a well-founded suspicion of results tampering or other similar frauds, the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance may precautionary rule:

- I - immediate suspension of bets and withholding of prizes related to the suspicious event;
- II - suspension or prohibition, of one or more operator agents, from betting on intercurrent or specific events happening during the suspicious competition, game or dispute, including on the specific prediction of the final result; and
- III - other restrictive measures aimed at avoiding or mitigating the consequences of practices that violate sport integrity.

§ 1. The Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance will communicate about the signs of infractions to the corresponding inspection bodies of the competent authorities of public administration.

§ 2. In cases when the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance understands that the signs identified are enough to determine an infraction, the communication referred to on §1 may happen before the initiation or judgement of the sanctioning administrative proceeding.

## CHAPTER VI

## ON THE INITIATION OF THE SANCTIONING ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEEDING

Art. 22.- The Undersecretary of Monitoring and Investigation of the Secretariat of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance, upon determining, in the exercise of investigation competencies, the occurrence of signs of punishable administrative infractions by the terms of applying legislation to fixed odds betting lottery modality, will initiate the sanctioning administrative proceeding for verification, excepting the hypothesis provided on art. 43 of Law No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023.

Single paragraph. The sanctioning administrative proceeding must:

- I - be recorded on the Electronic System of Information - SEI and classified by its corresponding legal hypothesis; and
- II - follow the procedure on the specific regulations provided by the Office of Prizes and Bets of the Ministry of Finance.

## CHAPTER VII

## FINAL AND TRANSITIONAL PROVISIONS

Art. 23.- The rules provided for on this Ordinance shall produce its effects since 1st of January of 2025.

Art. 24.- This Ordinance comes into force on the day it is published.
