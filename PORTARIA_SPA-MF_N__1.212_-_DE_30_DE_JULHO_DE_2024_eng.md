# ORDINANCE SPA/MF No. 1,212, OF 30th OF JULY OF 2024

**FEDERAL OFFICIAL GAZETTE**  
Published on: 31/07/2024 | Edition: 146 | Section: 1 | Page: 63  
Entity: Ministry of Finance/Secretariat of Prizes and Betting

**ORDINANCE SPA/MF No. 1,212, OF 30th OF JULY OF 2024**

Establishes procedures for the transfer of resources of the collection from fixed odds betting lotteries, as treated by §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, and other measures.

THE SECRETARY OF PRIZES AND BETTING OF THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE, in the exercise of powers bestowed upon him by art. 55, item I, paragraph “d”, of Annex I of Decree No. 11,907, of 30th of January of 2024, and having regard to Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, and to Law No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023, rules:

### Art. 1.-

This Ordinance establishes procedures for the transfer of resources of the collection from fixed odds betting lotteries, as treated by §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018.

### Art. 2.-

The transfers in favour of the Single Account of the National Treasury must be executed through Inland Revenue Collection Document (DARF - Documento de Arrecadação de Receitas Federais).

#### Single paragraph.

Transfers in favour of the Single Account of the National Treasury are those provided by items II, III, paragraphs “h” and “i”, IV-A, V, VI, VIII and IX of §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018.

### Art. 3.-

The collection of resources provided by items II, III, paragraphs “h” and “i”, V, VIII and IX of §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, must be executed through DARF with revenue code (código de receita): 5862 (PARTICIP.UNIÃO REC.LOTER.APOSTAS QUOTA FIXA)

### Art. 4.-

The collection of resources provided by items IV-A and VI of §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, must be executed through DARF with revenue code (código de receita): 9197 (CONTRIB.S/RECEITA LOTERIAS APOSTAS QUOTA FIXA).

### Art. 5.-

The betting operator agent is responsible for selecting the correct revenue code on DARF.

### Art. 6.-

The betting operator agent that fails to transfer the collections provided by §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, in compliance with the provisions of this Ordinance, is subject to civil, administrative and criminal liability.

#### Single paragraph.

The transfers of resources from the betting operator agent are subjected to the general principles of the public administration and accountability, through monthly reports and inspection of the Secretariat of Prizes and Betting.

### Art. 7.-

In case of expired prizes, when the bettor loses their right to receive their prize or request a refund, the amounts of the prizes will be collected on the Single Account of the National Treasury, observing what is stated in art. 32 of Law No. 14,790, of 29th of December of 2023, and in act of the Secretariat of the Inland Revenue of Brazil.

### Art. 8.-

The form of distribution of the transfers provided by paragraph “a” of item III of §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, will be regulated on a specific norm.

### Art. 9.-

The form of distribution of the transfers provided by paragraphs “b” through “g” and “j”, of item III, and item VII, of §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, will be issued by specific guidelines of the Secretariat of Prizes and Betting.

### Art. 10.-

The betting operator agent must rightfully keep the documentation demonstrating the transfers in favour of the Treasury and legal beneficiaries at the disposal of the Secretariat of Prizes and Betting for 5 (five) years.

### Art. 11.-

The transfers treated by §1-A of art. 30 of Law No. 13,756, of 12th of December of 2018, consider the proceeds of revenues resulting from all modalities of fixed odds betting, virtual or physical, that involve real sporting events as well as virtual online games.

### Art. 12.-

This Ordinance comes into force on the day it is published, and shall produce its effects since 1st of January of 2025.

REGIS ANDERSON DUDENA
